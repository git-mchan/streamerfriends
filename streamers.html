Meet The Streamers

The following questions were asked:
1. How often do you stream? How long do you usually stream?
2. How do you prefer to showcase yourself and why? How did you feel using any of these types if applicable? (PNGTuber, Vtuber, real face cam, etc)
3. If you’ve used multiple types of avatars, which one do you think your viewers preferred? would you use it more if your viewers liked it best even if it’s out of your comfort zone?
4. What are the types of things you stream?
5. What is your goal(s) when streaming? Some Ex. To meet new people; Something to do like a hobby; Create a community unique to you… etc
6. Since I’m mainly doing research on spycant, chanreee, addddistar, which are all friends. Would you say your communities intertwine with each other or are completely different? What would you say about the vibes about each other’s community, including your own.
